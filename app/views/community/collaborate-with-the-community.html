Overview of community collaboration

The MoJ Design System is a collaboration between the community of users and the design system team. Anyone can get involved to help improve and grow the design system by:

  • adding new 'community components'
  • providing more information about community components
  • offering feedback on components and patterns
  • taking part in discussions with other community members

What is a community component?

A community component is a new component submitted by a person working outside of the MoJ design system team.

What makes it a community component is that it can be added without all the information available. This removes the barrier of entry to submitting new components to the design system. And allows the community of users to continue developing these components together.

Who can submit new community components to the design system?

Anyone can submit new components to the design system.

The team encourages people from the MoJ and other government communities to get involved in developing the design system we all use.

There are no limitations on who can submit components to the design system or what those components are.

The team wants to hear from you

Adding a new community component
